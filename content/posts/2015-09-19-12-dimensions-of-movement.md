[PDF download](/pdfs/tq_InnerPages_0001.pdf)

12 dimensions:

* Heavy - Light
* Isolated - Integrated
* Low - Tall
* Indirect - Direct
* Open - Closed
* Linear - Circular
* Activated Space - Ignored Space
* Sturdy - Falling
* Constriction - Extension
* Angular - Parallel
* High Intensity - Inactive
* Quick - Sustained
